Taco Salad

Description:

Taco salad is a delicious, healthy and nutrious food, of Texmex origins; in a mixture of ingredients that is quick and easy to prepare!

Ingredients

  • ground beef
  • frijoles
  • tomatoes
  • onion
  • peppers
  • avocado
  • cheese
  • chips
  • spices
  • anything else you desire!

Steps

  1. brown the ground beef on stove
  2. dice the veggies while beef cooks
  3. drain fat from beef, then add spices and simmer
  4. pile onto plate portion of veggies, beans, chips, cheese, avocado, and top with ground beef
  5. Enjoy!
